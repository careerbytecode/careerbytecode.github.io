The Story

How we got here

CareerByteCode began as a small circle of practitioners who refused to teach what they hadn't shipped. Online sessions came first - free, hands-on, late-night labs where engineers from across the world rebuilt clusters, broke production replicas, and learned by doing.

Somewhere between the 30th LinkedIn live and the first time a learner flew across the country just to meet their mentor in person, we realised the screen wasn't enough. The hugs at the door, the whiteboard arguments, the shared chai between two strangers who'd debug a Terraform plan together - those needed a real room.

So we started showing up in cities. Renting halls, partnering with sponsors who got it, and inviting the community to one full day of demos, conversations, and the kind of clarity you only get face-to-face. This page is where those days live on.
