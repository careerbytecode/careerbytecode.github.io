How payments work on CareerByteCode

All payments processed via Razorpay Connect Marketplace. CareerByteCode never holds or controls funds.

StepWhat HappensHandled By
1Customer pays the listed price plus applicable taxes at checkoutRazorpay
2Razorpay Tax calculates VAT, GST, sales tax, or no tax based on customer locationRazorpay Tax
3Razorpay deducts its processing feeRazorpay
4Net revenue is split automatically to all stakeholder connected accountsRazorpay Connect
5Platform share collected as Razorpay application feeRazorpay Connect
6Each stakeholder receives their share directly in their Razorpay accountRazorpay
Refund Policy: Three-tier framework - digital content waiver (EU Article 16(m)) / service completion / 14-day full refund for marketplace services. All refunds processed via Razorpay.
Important: CareerByteCode is the deemed supplier for tax purposes. Razorpay Tax handles location-based tax. The platform never holds customer or seller funds.

operational terms

Payments, refunds & key rules

TermDetails
Payment ProcessingAll payments via Razorpay Connect Marketplace. Split automatically at point of transaction.
Razorpay AccountsAll stakeholders must have a Razorpay connected account to receive payouts.
Tax CollectionRazorpay Tax calculates and collects applicable tax based on customer location.
Refund: Digital ContentTier 1: Checkout waiver per EU Article 16(m). Customer consents to immediate access and waives 14-day withdrawal.
Refund: Live ServicesTier 2: 14-day withdrawal right until service is performed. Express consent for early performance.
Refund: MarketplaceTier 3: Full 14-day withdrawal. Full refund, no deductions. Platform absorbs Razorpay fee.
Abuse ProtectionConsumption tracking, pattern detection, account restrictions for repeat abusers.
Fund ControlCareerByteCode never holds, stores, or controls customer or seller funds at any point.
