How Payments Work on CareerByteCode

All payments processed via Stripe Connect Marketplace. CareerByteCode never holds or controls funds.

StepWhat HappensHandled By
1Customer pays the listed price plus applicable taxes at checkoutStripe
2Stripe Tax calculates VAT, GST, sales tax, or no tax based on customer locationStripe Tax
3Stripe deducts its processing feeStripe
4Net revenue is split automatically to all stakeholder connected accountsStripe Connect
5Platform share collected as Stripe application feeStripe Connect
6Each stakeholder receives their share directly in their Stripe accountStripe
Refund Policy: Three-tier framework — digital content waiver (EU Article 16(m)) / service completion / 14-day full refund for marketplace services. All refunds processed via Stripe.
Important: CareerByteCode is the deemed supplier for tax purposes. Stripe Tax handles location-based tax. The platform never holds customer or seller funds.

Payments, Refunds & Key Rules

TermDetails
Payment ProcessingAll payments via Stripe Connect Marketplace. Split automatically at point of transaction.
Stripe AccountsAll stakeholders must have a Stripe connected account to receive payouts.
Tax CollectionStripe Tax calculates and collects applicable tax based on customer location.
Refund: Digital ContentTier 1: Checkout waiver per EU Article 16(m). Customer consents to immediate access and waives 14-day withdrawal.
Refund: Live ServicesTier 2: 14-day withdrawal right until service is performed. Express consent for early performance.
Refund: MarketplaceTier 3: Full 14-day withdrawal. Full refund, no deductions. Platform absorbs Stripe fee.
Abuse ProtectionConsumption tracking, pattern detection, account restrictions for repeat abusers.
Fund ControlCareerByteCode never holds, stores, or controls customer or seller funds at any point.
