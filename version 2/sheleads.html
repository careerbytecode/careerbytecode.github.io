✦ Before You Start

Mandatory Program Rules

Every participant must follow ALL rules without exception. These ensure maximum learning and a consistent professional experience.

💻

Device

Laptop or Desktop ONLY. Mobile phones are not suitable for class work.

🌐

Browser

Google Chrome ONLY for all class work and assignments.

🔒

Chrome Usage

No WhatsApp Web, personal Gmail, or social media inside your CareerByteCode Chrome profile.

📡

Internet

Stable broadband or Wi-Fi only. No mobile hotspot during class.

🔑

Digital Identity

One Gmail · One Username · Same handle everywhere. Consistency is non-negotiable.

🗄️

Credentials

Store your username, password, and recovery email safely at all times.

🖥️

Screen Sharing

Know how to share full screen and a Chrome-only window before every class.

🌎

Africa Exception

Dev.to alone is acceptable if stable broadband is unavailable in your region.
